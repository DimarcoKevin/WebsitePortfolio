About Me

    In High School, I wasn't exactly sure what I wanted to do in the future. I chose to take a General Business program, so I would have a good knowledge of accounting, math, and project management. These skills would be beneficial no matter which direction my career led me.

    After college, I started learning Java which peaked a growing interest in software development. In my free time, I started out making small text-based applications. Realizing I was not learning at the pace I wanted to, I decided to re-enroll in school; This time for Computer Sciences.

    I have enjoyed learning many languages and technologies along the way. Now I know I want to pursue a lifetime career of learning and growing with software.
